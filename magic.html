Magical Entertainment

Magical Entertainment is also a very important part of any children’s party, and in combination with a Punch and Judy Show usually will manage to retain interest over an hour long period without any problem…

I normally cater for a wide range of age groups usually from 4 upwards … Parties can of course be difficult and stressful for parents to organise, but with a few helpful tips and words of advice this can be significantly reduced….. !

You will see I have included these on this website along with “ A few Questions Answered” Parties of course can be performed at a wide range of venues …

My show is very flexible and can fit quite comfortably into most modern homes, halls or outdoor venues without problems… !

Please contact me for further details and bookings …!

I normally work within the West Midlands Area but will travel by negotiation.

 

Magic, especially when presented in conjunction with a "Punch and Judy" show can provide a wonderful experience for young and old alike.

Audience participation is activley encouraged and at a birthday party especially by the birthday child. Usually a small gift is presented to the child during the show with a rousing chorus of "We wish you a happy birthday" if appropriate.

A complete range of magical effects are used depending on the age range of the children and for very small children the magic is of course mainly visual.

 

Derek has a wide experience of presenting Magic and will  endeavour to make your party  go with a bang… !

   

 Still photography during the show is no problem but the complete recording of the “Magic and Punch and Judy Show” in it’s entirety is not normally permitted… I am sure that   you will all appreciate an understand this and respect my wishes…

   

So let me  have the opportunity to provide the Children's Entertainment at you next Party  or Event and I  am sure that you will not be disappointed … Many of my engagements come from personal  recommendations  and I have entertained many children from the same families over  quite a few years… !

   

Please  contact me for  further details and bookings …!
